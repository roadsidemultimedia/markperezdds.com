About El Cerrito DDS, Mark Perez

Dr. Perez is a master smilest. For over 25 years, Mark Perez, DDS in El Cerrito, has made it his passion and dedication to create smiles that look naturally beautiful and healthy!

Long before the makeover craze became popular, Dr. Perez was mastering his artistic and advanced restorative and cosmetic dentistry skills to create smiles of surpassing beauty and radiant health.

Dr. Perez, our El Cerrito DDS, has world-class credentials, including:

  • A graduate of the prestigious University of California, San Francisco School of Dentistry in 1983.
  • A graduate of the Seattle Institute For Advanced Dental Education
  • A graduate of the continuing Education Program UCLA Center for Esthetic Dentistry
  • A Willard Flemming Scholar from the University of California, San Francisco School of Dentistry
  • Faculty member of the year 1991 UCSF School of Dentistry
  • Former Faculty member at UCLA Center for Esthetic Dentistry
  • Former Faculty member at UCSF School of Dentistry Department of Restorative Dentistry
  • Former Instructor/Mentor at Spear Institute of Advanced Dental Education of Seattle and Scottsdale
  • Former Instructor/Mentor at Scottsdale Center for Dentistry
  • Former Member Spear Faculty Club

Our El Cerrito DDS creates natural beauty with exacting skill, expert knowledge and compassionate care. You couldn't put the care of your special smile into better hands than Dr. Mark Perez. Your search is over!

Naturally Beautiful Dentistry

Based on a treatment philosophy that "all dentistry should look natural," Dr. Perez has completed rigorous supplemental training involving hundreds of hours of continuing education in cosmetic dentistry, implantology, and advanced restorative dentistry. This superior training from the most highly esteemed educators from all over the country puts his cosmetic and restorative work into a class by itself.

These sophisticated credentials and his dedication to learning at the highest levels available anywhere have made him a much sought after instructor by other cosmetic and restorative dentists from all over the world.

Involving years of dedication and hundreds of hours of education and hands-on training, Dr. Perez has completed numerous programs at the prestigious UCLA Center for Esthetic Dentistry, the Seattle Institute of Advanced Dental Education and the world-renowned Spear Institute for Advanced Dental Education in Scottsdale, Arizona. Having completed advanced education in: 

  • Age-Reversing Dentistry
  • Porcelain Veneers, Crowns and Bridges
  • Smile Design, Oral Face Lifts, Cosmetic Makeovers
  • TMJ (Bite Disorders)
  • Occlusion (Diagnosis of Bite Pathology and Solutions)
  • Periodontics (Treatment of Gums and Supporting Bone)
  • Instant Orthodontics
  • Implants
  • Root Canals
  • Full Mouth Restoration
  • Expert Bonding

In addition to his unequaled skill, experience, and education, another thing that patients rave about is his extraordinary gentleness and approachability. Your comfort is his number one concern. He has dedicated his El Cerrito DDS practice to treating only one patient at a time, where his complete focus and attention is on you. There is no rushing or distractions.

Because of his reputation as "one of the best", Dr. Perez has been invited by Dr. Frank Spear (an internationally renowned prosthodontist, periodontist and educator) to join the elite Spear Faculty Club. This exclusive club includes a small number of highly trained dentists from all over the world dedicated to practicing dentistry at an elevated level. They are committed to providing their patients with the very best! With his expertise in dental solutions, you can smile with beauty all the time.
