Berkeley Area Teeth Whitening

Brilliant, dazzling, healthy embody an unforgettable smile. Making your smile memorable is easy, quick and very affordable with Berkeley area teeth whitening. Professional whitening by expert dentist, Dr. Perez can effectively remove all the stains and yellowing caused by smoking or drinking tea, coffee, and wine; leaving you with a healthy, bright smile.

The many El Cerrito and Berkeley teeth whitening options offered by our office make it convenient for anyone to bring back the dazzle to their smile. Whether you prefer to whiten for a shorter period during the day or chose a longer treatment to use at night, you will be thrilled with the difference 1 week will make. Fresher, brighter and whiter!

Customizing your whitening kit is the key to success. Experienced, cosmetic dentist, Dr. Perez can create the perfect solution for you, giving you the kind of results that come only from professionally supervised teeth bleaching.

The Perfect Whitening Solution for You

Teeth bleaching can be a great temporary solution for discoloration, unlike crowns, veneers or bonding, which provide permanent whitening. It easy to do, provides quick results and can make a dramatic change in the appearance of your smile.

Dr. Perez can custom create mouth trays for your personal whitening kit. These trays are designed to fit you perfectly, giving you full contact and coverage with the professional grade whitening gel you will use in them. This avoids the uneven whitening results that are often seen with over-the-counter kits.

You can whiten during the day or at night while you sleep. There are a variety of different whitening agents to use and Dr. Perez will choose the most effective for you. He may also recommend a teeth whitening treatment before certain cosmetic procedures such as porcelain veneers to ensure an evenly white result.

Because this is a temporary solution, you will need to touch up your whitening from time to time. During your regular cleaning visits with our office, we will check for any dimming or yellowing and supply bleaching refills for your at-home Berkeley area teeth whitening kit.
