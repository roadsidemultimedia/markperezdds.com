Gum Contouring - El Cerrito Dental Care Creates Symmetry

Gum contouring can make your smile look healthier and more attractive by framing and complementing your teeth, like a quality frame is to a painting.

Gum contouring could be the cosmetic improvement to your smile that you are looking for. El Cerrito dental care by Dr. Perez uses this solution to help teeth look longer, and take away a “gummy smile”. With this procedure, your true smile will be revealed as your teeth are no longer overshadowed by excessive gums.

Some may have uneven gums that are disproportionate. Dr. Perez's El Cerrito dental care can quickly remedy this with minor gum sculpting and adjustments. Gum contouring may also play a role in enhancing your smile design with porcelain veneers.

Dr. Perez and his dental team use state-of-the-art equipment to artistically realign or straighten gums. In only a few minutes you can have a finely sculpted gum line! Dental bridges or an oral face lift are the perfect compliment to our gum contouring services.

Our technology is conservative, with very little discomfort afterward. Local anesthetic is all that is required. Why wait? Our highly skilled dentist can create a healthy and attractive gum line quickly and easily!
