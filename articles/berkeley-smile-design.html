The Berkeley Area Smile Design Process

The goal of Berkeley area smile design dentist Dr. Perez, is to redesign a smile that fits you individually, that is strong and natural looking, and, most importantly, makes you happy! To aid in this, our talented dentist Dr. Perez has custom designed a specialty instrument called a Tooth-Size Indicator for use in his cutting edge dentistry. It measures the size and shape of your face and shows what the size of your teeth should be in relation to your facial features for optimum beauty.

Providing years of quality dentistry, Dr. Perez uses modeling materials to form a mock-up of the perfect design for your smile right on the surface of your teeth. In less that 15 minutes, you can have a preview of your smile that you can show your friends and family.

A Master Artist for Lasting Beauty

Once your design blueprint is decided, it will be sent it to our lab to have your porcelain restorations created. Our dentistry uses porcelain veneers are thin shells of porcelain material that are bonded directly over the tooth surface by the dentist to create a new smile.

Our porcelain veneers are crafted by an AACD (American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry) recognized Premier Ceramist. His goal is to create tooth restorations that mimic perfectly the look, color, luster and translucency of your own natural teeth. But he is also focused on creating restorations that are strong, functional and fitting correctly to create a comfortable and optimally working bite. Your smile must both look and feel good!

Our patients just can't believe how in only 2 visits, their appearance, and the way they feel about themselves can be so dramatically changed. Your dramatic transformation can start by arranging a consultation for your Smile Design, with our Berkeley area smile design team.
